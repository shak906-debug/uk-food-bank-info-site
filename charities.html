Major Food Charities

A number of national charities work tirelessly to address food poverty and hunger across the UK. They not only provide meals and food parcels but also campaign for systemic change. Below is an overview of some of the main organisations and what they do.

The Trussell Trust

The Trussell Trust supports a nationwide network of over 1,200 food bank centres. Its vision is for a future where no one in the UK needs a food bank. The Trust provides nutritionally balanced emergency food and support to people facing crises and campaigns to end the need for food banks entirely【139774249184268†L42-L56】. Between April and September 2021 the network distributed around 5,100 emergency food parcels every day【139774249184268†L58-L61】.

FareShare

FareShare tackles both food waste and hunger. Millions of tonnes of perfectly edible food are wasted by the UK food industry every year. At the same time, millions of people struggle to afford to eat. FareShare addresses these issues by redistributing surplus food that would otherwise go to waste to frontline organisations supporting people facing hunger【56581690013979†L169-L174】. The charity notes that around 11 million people in the UK are struggling to get enough to eat【56581690013979†L182-L199】, and there are over 4 million tonnes of good‑to‑eat food wasted each year — enough for an estimated 10 billion meals【56581690013979†L190-L197】.

Magic Breakfast

Magic Breakfast works with schools across England and Scotland to ensure that no child starts the day too hungry to learn. The charity provides more than 300,000 breakfasts every school day and offers expert advice to tackle morning hunger【130247994050415†L278-L283】. It runs research‑led programmes demonstrating that school breakfasts improve pupils’ mental wellbeing and educational attainment【130247994050415†L343-L359】.

The Felix Project

The Felix Project is London’s largest food redistribution charity. It rescues high‑quality surplus food that would otherwise go to waste and redistributes it to over 1,200 community organisations across the capital【732076860572306†L45-L50】. The charity runs initiatives like Felix’s Kitchen and Multibank to provide meals and household essentials to families in need, and it works with volunteers and partners across the city to fight food waste and hunger【732076860572306†L45-L52】.
