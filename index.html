Supporting Communities Across the UK

Millions of people across the UK rely on food banks every year. This site provides a comprehensive directory of food banks, charities and support schemes to help families, children and the vulnerable access food assistance and other vital services.

Find a Food Bank

Why This Matters

  • 14 million+ people in the UK live in poverty, including around 4.5 million children.
  • The Trussell Trust’s network distributed over 5,100 emergency food parcels every day between April and September 2021.
  • Charities like Magic Breakfast provide over 300,000 breakfasts daily to children and young people in England and Scotland.

About This Website

Finding help should be easy. Our interactive map lets you search for food banks near you and filter by network or region. We also highlight major food charities and government programmes that provide support through meals, vouchers and educational initiatives. Whether you’re looking for assistance or want to donate or volunteer, you’ll find everything you need here.
